Privacy Policy

Application: SellerResponder Marketplace
Package: com.idmasoft.robotrespondermsg
Last updated: May 13, 2026

Summary: SellerResponder is an auto-reply assistant designed for online marketplace sellers. All reply rules, message history, and app settings are stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data or your customers' conversations.

1. Who We Are

SellerResponder is developed and maintained by IdmaSoft. If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at: idmasoft.dev@gmail.com

2. What This App Does

SellerResponder helps sellers automatically respond to marketplace-related messages received through supported messaging applications using Android notification features.

The app reads incoming notifications through Android's Notification Listener Service and sends pre-configured automatic replies on your behalf. All reply rules are created and managed locally by you on your own device.

3. Local Processing

All automatic reply detection, keyword matching, and message processing occur entirely on your device. SellerResponder does not send conversations, messages, or customer data to external servers, cloud systems, or AI services.

4. Data Stored On Your Device

The following information may be stored locally on your device only:

  • Marketplace rules: Titles of your listings and the automatic question-and-answer rules you configure.
  • Reply history: A local history of automatic replies, including timestamps, sender display names (as shown in notifications), received question text, and sent replies.
  • Application preferences: Settings such as delays, cooldowns, deduplication windows, notification preferences, and service states.
  • Built-in intent patterns: Internal keyword templates used to detect common seller questions (price, availability, delivery, etc.).

This information is never transmitted to IdmaSoft servers.

5. Notification Access Permission

SellerResponder requires Android's Notification Listener permission (android.permission.BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE) to detect incoming marketplace-related messages and generate automatic replies.

  • The app only processes notifications from supported messaging applications related to marketplace conversations.
  • Supported packages may include: com.facebook.orca, com.facebook.mlite, and com.facebook.katana.
  • Notifications from unrelated applications are ignored.
  • Message content is never uploaded to cloud services or external servers.
  • Reply history stored locally on your device can be deleted at any time from within the app.

6. Other Permissions Used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Used to display service status notifications and optional notifications when automatic replies are sent.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE: Required to keep the auto-reply service running reliably in the background.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: Optionally requested to reduce interruptions caused by aggressive battery-saving systems on some devices.

7. Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics

SellerResponder uses Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics provided by Google to improve app stability and reliability.

These services may collect:

  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs.
  • Device model, Android version, and app version.
  • Anonymous usage statistics and app performance information.

SellerResponder does not send:

  • Customer messages
  • Sender names
  • Marketplace conversations
  • Reply contents
  • Personally identifiable information

Firebase services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

8. Third-Party Services

SellerResponder interacts with supported messaging applications exclusively through Android's official notification and reply APIs.

SellerResponder is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Meta Platforms, Inc.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All locally stored data can be deleted by the user at any time from within the app.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local application data from the device.
  • Firebase crash data retention is managed according to Google's Firebase policies.

10. Children's Privacy

SellerResponder is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
